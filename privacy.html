Labas Learner Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Labas Learner is currently designed as a local, invite-only learning app for family and trusted tester feedback.

Data Collected

The app does not create accounts, collect names, collect email addresses, track location, use analytics, or send lesson answers to a server.

Lesson content is bundled inside the app as local JSON. XP and practice progress currently live only in the running app session.

Audio

Lithuanian speech uses the Android device text-to-speech engine. The app does not upload speech audio to a paid AI service.

Speaking practice is self-check only. The app does not record microphone audio.

Payments

The current test version does not include payments, subscriptions, in-app purchases, or advertising.

Credits

The current picture practice uses emoji-style symbols rendered by the user's device. Local feedback sounds are bundled with the app.

If future versions add third-party image or audio libraries, this privacy policy and the in-app credits screen will be updated with the relevant licences and attribution.
